Level: Easy
Total: 5 min
Yield: 1 cup

Ingredients

- 1/4 cup vinegar
- 3/4 cup olive oil
- 2 to 3 teaspoons Dijon mustard
- 1 small clove garlic, minced
- 1 1/4 teaspoon salt and pepper

Directions

Whisk together all ingredients in a bowl until combined and blended well. 
Use right away, or refrigerate in a sealed container for up to 3 days.
